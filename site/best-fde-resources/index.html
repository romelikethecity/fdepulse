Best Resources for Forward Deployed Engineers in 2026

Forward deployed engineering started at Palantir and has spread to 50+ companies. The role sits between product engineering and customer success, with FDEs embedded directly with customers to build custom solutions.

This list covers what exists today. Job boards, engineering blogs, communities, and career resources for people who build software at the customer's site, not in the home office.

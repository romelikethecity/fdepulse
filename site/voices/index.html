Top 25 Forward Deployed Engineer Voices of 2026

The most influential FDEs, customer engineers, and field engineers shaping the profession

Last updated: 2026-04-14 · 25 voices ranked

How We Ranked These Voices

Ranked by cross-list appearances, public writing, community impact, and relevance to Forward Deployed Engineering. Sources include Pragmatic Engineer, SVPG, company engineering blogs (Palantir, OpenAI, Anthropic, Scale AI), and independent analysis. We evaluated 40+ candidates and filtered for people who actively shape how FDE teams operate.

  • FDE relevance (required): Must contribute to the FDE/customer-embedded engineering profession.
  • Published work (30%): Books, newsletters, blog posts, talks that advance the discipline.
  • Industry impact (25%): Building FDE teams, investing in FDE companies, defining the role.
  • Content reach (25%): Newsletter subscribers, LinkedIn following, conference appearances.
  • Originality (20%): Original thinking about customer-embedded engineering.

Top 10 Leaders

The most recognized voices shaping Forward Deployed Engineering.

#1

Gergely Orosz

Author & Newsletter Writer at The Pragmatic Engineer

Newsletter700K SubscribersFDE Coverage

Runs The Pragmatic Engineer newsletter (700K+ subscribers) and wrote the definitive coverage of the FDE role. His analysis of how companies like Palantir, OpenAI, and Scale AI deploy engineers directly to customers shaped how the industry understands and values the role.

#2

Marty Cagan

Author & Partner at SVPG (Silicon Valley Product Group)

AuthorSVPGProduct Delivery

Author of 'Inspired,' 'Empowered,' and 'Transformed.' SVPG's coverage of the FDE model as a product delivery pattern helped legitimize the role beyond Palantir. His frameworks for embedded engineering teams influence how companies think about customer-facing technical roles.

#3

Shyam Sankar

CTO at Palantir

Palantir CTOFDE OriginEnterprise AI

Palantir's CTO and the architect of the original Forward Deployed Engineering model. Palantir invented the FDE role, and Sankar's vision of engineers embedded with customers solving their hardest problems became the template that every AI company now copies.

#4

Alexandr Wang

CEO & Founder at Scale AI

Scale AICEOFDE Employer

Built Scale AI into one of the largest employers of Forward Deployed Engineers outside of Palantir. Scale's FDE program, which deploys engineers to help enterprise customers implement AI at production scale, became the blueprint for the AI-era FDE role.

#5

Kevin Hartz

Partner at A16z / Eventbrite Founder

VCA16zFDE Thesis

A16z partner who has written and spoken about the FDE model as a competitive advantage for AI companies. His investment thesis on why FDEs create defensible moats influenced how startups think about customer-embedded engineering.

#6

Ali Ghodsi

CEO & Co-Founder at Databricks

DatabricksCEOField Engineering

Built Databricks' field engineering organization into one of the strongest customer-embedded technical teams in enterprise software. Databricks' approach to deploying engineers alongside customers during implementation became a model for the data/AI infrastructure space.

#7

Clément Delangue

CEO & Co-Founder at Hugging Face

Hugging FaceOpen SourceCommunity

Hugging Face's CEO who built a model where community engineers serve as de facto FDEs, helping enterprise customers deploy open-source AI models. His approach to customer-embedded support through open source is a new flavor of forward deployment.

#8

Will Larson

CTO & Author at Carta / Author of Staff Engineer

AuthorCareer LaddersEngineering Org

Author of 'Staff Engineer' and 'An Elegant Puzzle.' His writing on engineering career ladders, staff+ roles, and organizational design shapes how FDEs think about their career trajectory. The concept of 'staff engineer as force multiplier' maps directly to the FDE value proposition.

#9

Chip Huyen

Author & ML Engineer at Independent

ML SystemsAuthorProduction AI

Author of 'Designing Machine Learning Systems.' Her work on ML deployment, real-world ML challenges, and the gap between ML research and production systems speaks directly to the FDE mission of making AI work in the field, not just in the lab.

#10

Hamel Husain

ML Engineer & Educator at Independent (Ex-GitHub, Ex-Airbnb)

ML EngineeringLLMProduction AI

Former ML engineer at GitHub and Airbnb. His blog and teaching on practical ML engineering, LLM fine-tuning, and customer-facing AI implementation make him one of the most followed voices for engineers who deploy AI in production environments.

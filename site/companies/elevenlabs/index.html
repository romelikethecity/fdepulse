Forward Deployed Engineer at ElevenLabs

New York / Remote · 5-10 FDEs · $160,000 - $240,000

Overview

ElevenLabs is a leading AI voice synthesis company. Their FDEs deploy custom voice AI solutions for enterprise customers: media companies, gaming studios, accessibility platforms, and customer service operations. ElevenLabs' FDE team is small but growing fast as the company expands from consumer voice cloning to enterprise voice AI infrastructure. The role combines audio engineering domain knowledge with traditional FDE skills.

What FDEs Do at ElevenLabs

ElevenLabs FDEs build custom voice AI integrations for enterprise clients. Typical work includes: deploying real-time voice synthesis APIs in customer applications, building custom voice models for specific use cases (character voices for games, branded voices for customer service), integrating voice AI with existing customer workflows (IVR systems, content production pipelines), and optimizing latency and quality for production voice applications. The work is uniquely creative compared to most FDE roles.

Tech Stack

Python, TypeScript, ElevenLabs API, WebSockets (for real-time streaming), audio processing libraries, Docker, cloud infrastructure (AWS/GCP). FDEs need comfort with audio data: sample rates, codecs, latency optimization, and voice quality evaluation. ML knowledge (voice synthesis, TTS models) is helpful but not required at the same depth as research roles.

Interview Process

ElevenLabs FDE interviews are 4 rounds: recruiter screen, coding (practical API and streaming work), system design (voice AI deployment scenario), and a customer scenario plus team fit round. The company moves fast (2 weeks to offer). They look for engineers who can ship quickly and are excited about voice AI specifically. Demonstrating familiarity with the ElevenLabs product in the interview is a strong signal.

Culture & Work-Life

Fast-moving startup culture with a small, tight-knit team. ElevenLabs has raised significant funding and is growing rapidly. FDEs have high autonomy and direct impact on enterprise revenue. The team is distributed (NYC and remote). Work-life balance depends on customer deployment intensity. Compensation includes meaningful pre-IPO equity.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need audio engineering experience for ElevenLabs FDE?

Not required but helpful. ElevenLabs trains FDEs on voice AI specifics. Strong software engineering skills plus interest in audio/voice technology is sufficient. If you've worked with audio APIs, real-time streaming, or media processing, highlight that experience.

What is ElevenLabs FDE salary?

Base salary ranges from $160,000 to $240,000. Total compensation including pre-IPO equity can be significant given ElevenLabs' rapid growth and high valuation. Early FDE hires stand to benefit most from equity appreciation.

Is ElevenLabs FDE remote?

Yes, ElevenLabs offers remote FDE positions. The company is distributed by design. Some customer deployments may require periodic travel, but fully remote work is supported. This makes ElevenLabs one of the more remote-friendly FDE employers.

How does ElevenLabs FDE compare to OpenAI FDE?

Both deploy AI products for enterprise customers. ElevenLabs FDEs specialize in voice AI (synthesis, cloning, real-time streaming). OpenAI FDEs work with language models (ChatGPT Enterprise, custom GPTs, RAG). ElevenLabs is smaller (more startup energy, more autonomy) while OpenAI is larger (more structure, higher compensation). If voice/audio AI excites you specifically, ElevenLabs is the better fit.

What types of customers do ElevenLabs FDEs work with?

Media and entertainment companies (character voices for games and films), customer service platforms (AI voice agents), accessibility organizations (text-to-speech for visually impaired users), content creators (podcast and audiobook production), and enterprise communication platforms. The customer diversity means FDEs see a wide range of deployment scenarios.

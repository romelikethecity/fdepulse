Caylent

Forward Deployed AI Strategist

$185K - $210K Remote Mid Posted May 06, 2026
Apply on Indeed All FDE Jobs

Role Description

Caylent is a cloud native services company that helps organizations bring the best out of their people and technology using Amazon Web Services (AWS). We provide a full-range of AWS services including workload migrations and modernization, cloud native application development, DevOps, data engineering, security and compliance, and everything in between.

At Caylent, our people always come first. We are a global company and operate fully remote with employees in Canada, the United States, and Latin America. We celebrate the culture of each of our team members and foster a community of technological curiosity. Come talk to us to learn more about what it means to be a Caylien!

About This Role -------------------

Caylent's Anthropic Enablement & Engineering (AE2) practice is a dedicated AI services team built in partnership with Anthropic. We were Anthropic's first pure-play delivery partner, and are working closely with them to define the next evolution of Software Development.

The Forward Deployed AI Strategist leads the work that defines what transformation looks like before a single Architect is deployed. You own blueprint engagements — comprehensive assessments of how an organization builds software across the full SDLC and PDLC, where AI can change the economics, and what a credible multi-phase roadmap looks like. You then lead the execution programs that follow, coordinating Agentic Architects across parallel workstreams and driving outcomes against the plan you set.

On engagements where scope and timeline call for it, you step in part-time as an Agentic Architect.

Your clients are PE portfolio companies, growth-stage software businesses, and enterprise engineering organizations where transformation decisions are made at the C-suite and board level.

What You'll Do ------------------

Transformation Strategy

  • Lead AI transformation blueprint engagements: current-state assessment across SDLC and PDLC, gap analysis, and prioritized initiative roadmap
  • Run executive discovery with CTOs, CPOs, and VP Engineering to surface strategic goals, constraints, and success criteria
  • Assess AI adoption posture across engineering and product functions — tooling, workflows, org design, delivery practices
  • Define initiative sequencing: what to address first, what to automate, where to standardize
  • Develop board-ready transformation cases that connect AI investment to PE value creation metrics — revenue per head, time-to-feature, delivery predictability
  • Facilitate executive alignment workshops that move stakeholders from awareness to decision

Execution Leadership

  • Lead multi-team AI transformation programs following blueprint delivery
  • Coordinate Agentic Architects across parallel workstreams; translate roadmap priorities into sprint-level milestones and delivery plans
  • Own the executive-sponsor relationship through delivery close
  • Step into Agentic Architect responsibilities on engagements where hands-on execution is required

Business Development

  • Shape follow-on scope throughout blueprint delivery — by handoff you have a working hypothesis for the execution program
  • Present the execution proposal at blueprint readout
  • Surface expansion signals across the client organization and route them to the account team

Internal Contribution

  • Codify transformation methodology: translate blueprint and execution patterns into reusable frameworks for the practice
  • Mentor Agentic Architects on executive engagement, strategic framing, and scoping

What We're Looking For --------------------------

Required

  • Significant experience across 12+ years across software development, technical consulting, or a combination, with demonstrable knowledge / experience on both SDLC and PDLC sides of a software organization
  • Proven success 5+ years leading transformation, strategy, or technical advisory engagements with engineering and product organizations
  • Hands-on experience with Claude Code or equivalent AI-assisted development tools in a real workflow
  • Experience building and presenting technology transformation roadmaps to C-level and board audiences
  • Strong working knowledge of modern SDLC (CI/CD, DevOps, cloud-native) and product development (agile delivery, roadmapping, product operations)
  • Experience sizing and sequencing multi-team, multi-quarter transformation programs
  • Prior professional services delivery experience with scope ownership and deliverable accountability

Preferred

  • Certified Claude Architect
  • Background in PE or growth-equity-backed software companies — understand value creation timelines and board expectations
  • Experience leading programs that span engineering and product simultaneously
  • Familiarity with developer experience and delivery metrics frameworks (DORA, DevEx, SPACE)
  • Experience designing AI governance and adoption guardrails for enterprise organizations
  • Direct experience building or operating agentic AI systems in production

Who Thrives Here --------------------

  • Thinks in roadmaps, not tasks. You see where an organization needs to be 12 months from now and can build a credible path to get there. Strategy that isn't accountable to outcomes is not strategy.
  • Can still do the work. You use Claude for all of your work. You'll step into Agentic Architect engagements when the situation calls for it. Seniority here means broader scope, not distance from delivery.
  • Credible at the board level. You've presented transformation cases to PE sponsors and C-suites and know how to connect technical decisions to business value without hand-waving.
  • Makes the methodology better. Every blueprint you run should make the next one faster. You feed patterns back into the practice rather than keeping them in your head.
  • Multiplies Architects. You can set direction, coordinate execution, and step back. You know what effective oversight looks like versus micromanagement.

This is a poor fit for purely advisory profiles. You own outcomes, not just recommendations.

Why This Team -----------------

  • Ground floor of something real: this BU is early, well-resourced, and anchored to Anthropic. You're writing the playbook, not inheriting one
  • Define the methodology: you are shaping how AI transformation gets scoped and delivered at scale. The frameworks you build here will drive how this practice grows
  • Proximity to the PE channel: your clients are PE portcos and enterprise engineering orgs at the frontier of AI adoption where transformation decisions are being made now
  • Revenue upside: Strategists who drive account expansion earn on revenue generated, not just delivery margin
  • AI-native by default: this team uses AI in how it works. Specification-First Engineering is how we move from prototype to production fast
  • Outcomes, not hours: advancement tied to delivery performance and account growth, not utilization

ConvergeProsperity - Product Forward Deployed Engineer, BankingSuite (Manager) - Innovation_Delivery_Transformation

$131K - $241K Stamford, CT, US Mid Posted April 30, 2026
Apply on Indeed All FDE Jobs

Role Description

The Team

Are you looking for a unique opportunity with both start-up spirit AND enterprise strength? Deloitte's Converge for FSI business offers both! We are looking for a talented individual with an innovative mindset to join the Converge for FSI team in our mission to develop differentiated financial services products that achieve product-market fit. This role is focused on BankingSuite, a managed composable banking platform that helps regional, universal, and community banks launch new digital product propositions & services and transform the digital customer experience. This is a great opportunity to be on the frontlines of Deloitte's innovation & product strategy while staying close to industry/sector priorities.

Recruiting for this role ends on 5/22/2026.

Work You'll Do

We are looking for a highly skilled and client-facing Forward Deployed Engineer (FDE) to sit at the intersection of engineering excellence and customer partnership. In this role, you will embed directly with our most strategic clients - particularly in the financial services sector - to understand their toughest technical challenges and help them design and implement world-class solutions using our platform. This is a high-impact, high-visibility role with direct influence on product direction.

You are equally comfortable whiteboarding microservices architecture with a client's principal engineer, running a hands-on technical workshop for a team of developers, and standing up a compelling proof-of-concept in days. You thrive in fast-moving, high-stakes environments and know how to earn the trust of deeply technical stakeholders.

Key Responsibilities

Client Engagement & Technical Advisory

  • Serve as the primary technical point of contact for strategic client accounts, building deep relationships with architects, engineers, and technical leaders
  • Lead discovery sessions to understand client architectures, pain points, and business objectives
  • Translate complex technical concepts into clear, compelling narratives for both technical and non-technical audiences

Solution Design & Architecture

  • Collaborate with client architects and internal engineering teams to design scalable, production-ready solutions
  • Provide authoritative guidance on microservices architecture, event-driven systems, and cloud-native deployment patterns
  • Review and advise on client system designs, identifying risks and recommending best practices

Proof of Concepts & Demos

  • Rapidly prototype and build POCs that demonstrate platform value in the context of a client's real-world use cases
  • Own and deliver polished, technically rigorous demos tailored to the client's specific technology stack and business domain
  • Maintain a library of reusable demo assets and reference implementations

Workshops & Enablement

  • Design and facilitate deep-dive technical workshops, hackathons, and onboarding bootcamps for client engineering teams
  • Produce high-quality technical documentation, runbooks, and integration guides
  • Accelerate client time-to-value by enabling their teams to build confidently and independently

Feedback & Internal Collaboration

  • Act as the voice of the customer - surface product gaps, integration friction, and feature requests to internal product and engineering teams
  • Partner with Sales, Solutions Architecture, and Product to shape the technical go-to-market strategy

The successful candidate will possess

Core Technical Skills

  • Deep expertise in Java and Spring Boot, including building production-grade microservices
  • Strong understanding of event-driven architecture - Kafka, event sourcing, CQRS, async messaging patterns
  • Hands-on experience with microservices design principles: service decomposition, API design, resiliency patterns, observability
  • Proficiency across multi-cloud environments, with AWS as a primary platform; working knowledge of Azure and GCP
  • Familiarity with containerization and orchestration (Docker, Kubernetes)

Emerging Technology

  • Exposure to Agentic AI frameworks and tooling - including LangGraph, LangChain, or similar orchestration frameworks
  • Practical understanding of LLM integration patterns, prompt engineering, and AI-powered application design
  • Interest in staying current with the rapidly evolving AI/ML landscape and translating it into client-relevant use cases

Soft Skills & Presence

  • Exceptional communicator - able to lead technical conversations with senior engineers and C-suite stakeholders alike
  • Strong facilitation skills for running workshops, design reviews, and working sessions
  • Self-directed, highly organized, and comfortable managing multiple client engagements simultaneously
  • Natural curiosity and a bias for building - you'd rather spin up a demo than write a slide about one

Additional Key Experience

  • Experience with CI/CD pipelines and DevSecOps practices
  • Contributions to open-source projects or public technical content (talks, blogs, repos)
  • Prior experience in a startup or high-growth SaaS environment
  • Relevant cloud certifications (AWS Solutions Architect, GCP Professional, etc.)

Required Qualifications

  • 8+ years of software engineering experience, with significant time spent in client-facing, solutions engineering, or field engineering roles; background in financial services (banking, capital markets, payments, insurance, or fintech) strongly preferred
  • Bachelor's Degree in Engineering, Business, or equivalent field; Advanced Degree is a plus - equivalent work experience may act as a substitute for degree requirements
  • Ability to travel 20-30%, on average, based on the work you do and the clients and industries/sectors you serve
  • Must be legally authorized to work in the United States without the need for employer sponsorship, now or at any time in the future

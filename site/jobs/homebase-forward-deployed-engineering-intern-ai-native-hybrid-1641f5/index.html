Homebase

Forward Deployed Engineering Intern, AI-Native (Hybrid)

Houston, TX, US Mid Posted April 26, 2026
Apply on Indeed All FDE Jobs

Role Description

Hi, Future Homie!

At Homebase, you’ll join a team that’s bold, fast-moving, and obsessed with helping small businesses thrive. We build with empathy, act with urgency, and take big swings that drive real-world impact. Here, every Homie shows up to raise the bar, support one another, and celebrate wins as a team.

We’re not just building an app—we’re building *unstoppable* teams. So what do you say, are you in?

This is a 3-month summer internship (June 2026 –August 2026) in Houston, Texas.

Your *Impact* Starts Here

We're looking for a Forward Deployed Engineering Intern who loves mapping real workflows, breaking down ambiguous problems, and shipping AI automations that directly improve how our internal teams operate. In this role, you'll embed primarily with our People Team (Talent, HR, and beyond), learn how their work gets done, and rapidly experiment with LLM-powered solutions that drive measurable impact.

This internship is a fit if you're excited about AI-first engineering, fast iteration, and operating at the front lines of the business. You'll help build the playbooks and tools that shape how Homebase adopts AI across the company.

These are the key ways you'll contribute and create impact in this role:

  • Embed with the People team, including Talent Acquisition and HR functions, to map workflows, surface pain points, and identify high-leverage AI automation opportunities that level up how we hire, onboard, and support our team.
  • Design, prototype, and ship AI-powered automations end-to-end, applying LLMs creatively to real business workflows in ambiguous, fast-changing environments.
  • Build full-stack solutions: From Python scripts and backend services to intuitive React interfaces — for internal teams to use in production.
  • Operate like a product-minded engineer: Run interviews, co-design solutions, and iterate based on real-world usage and feedback.
  • Measure impact rigorously: Ship changes that move metrics like rep talk time, ticket deflection, adoption, and operational efficiency.
  • Codify what works by creating reusable templates, playbooks, and documentation for future engineers and partner teams.
  • Experiment with AI tools to accelerate your own workflows and demonstrate AI fluency in your daily work (a Homebase expectation for all roles).

The Foundation for Success - These are the experiences and strengths that will set you up for success in this role:

  • Curiosity and enthusiasm for AI-first engineering, including hands-on experimentation with LLMs, agents, or automation tooling.
  • Experience with Python, JavaScript/TypeScript, or React, whether through coursework, personal projects, hackathons, or open-source contributions.
  • Comfortable navigating ambiguity, learning new systems quickly, and working directly with non-technical stakeholders.
  • A product mindset — ability to break down problems, scope solutions, and iterate quickly based on user feedback.
  • Strong communication skills for pairing with business partners and demoing work.
  • Genuine curiosity about People operations, HR tech, or talent workflows — or eagerness to learn how AI can transform the way organizations hire and grow their teams.
  • Currently enrolled in a college or university program and available for a summer internship (June–August).
  • Ability to collaborate in the Houston office on Tuesdays and Wednesdays.
  • Passion for helping small businesses and building tools that make teams more effective.

The Homie Way - These principles guide everything we do—from how we work and make decisions to how we show up for each other.

  • Be Customer Obsessed – Solve problems with empathy and creativity.
  • Move Fast, Learn Fast – Experiment, take action, and grow every day.
  • Own Your Impact – Think big, focus on what matters, and make decisions you stand behind.
  • Master Your Craft – Excellence fuels impact—show up, step up, and make your mark.
  • Win Together – Put goals over roles, lead with trust, and connect to our mission and each other.

What to Expect During the Interview Process

  • Meet the Talent Acquisition team, Mavel W.
  • Meet the Hiring Manager
  • Participate in a Talent Showcase
  • Background Check + Offer Stage
  • Welcome to the team, Homie

Belonging at Homebase - We're committed to fostering a welcoming space where every Homie can be their full self. Experience comes in many forms—so if you're excited about this role, even if you don’t meet 100% of the qualifications, we encourage you to apply!

*Homebase will consider qualified applicants with criminal histories in a manner consistent with the San Francisco Fair Chance Ordinance.*

Hey, We’re Homebase

Homebase is the everything app for hourly teams—built to simplify the day-to-day and superpower local businesses. With tools for scheduling, time clocks, payroll, communication, HR, and more, we help teams stay connected and in control. Today, over 150,000 small (but mighty) businesses rely on us to make work radically easier. Together, we’ve tracked over a billion hours for 2M+ workers—and we’re just getting started.

At Homebase, we celebrate diversity and are proud to be an equal opportunity employer. We welcome all candidates and do not discriminate based on any legally protected status. If you need accommodations during the hiring process, please let us know—we’re committed to ensuring fair and equitable access for all.

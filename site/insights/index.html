About This Data

FDE Pulse tracks job postings with "Forward Deployed Engineer" in the title across major job boards. Salary, location, skills, and company data are extracted from posting metadata and description text. The current dataset covers 141 active postings across 83 companies, with 111 postings disclosing compensation.

All market intelligence reports on this site are data-driven: claims are sourced directly from the posting dataset. We do not include opinion-based analysis, expert quotes, or projections beyond what the data supports. If you see a specific number, it came from a job posting.

Frequently Asked Questions

How fast is the Forward Deployed Engineer market growing?

Based on 134 active FDE postings tracked by FDE Pulse as of April 30, 2026, the market spans 70 companies and covers roles from entry-level to Director. The market has expanded well beyond Palantir's original model to include AI companies, consulting firms, enterprise SaaS, fintech, and more.

What is the median FDE salary in 2026?

The median base salary across 111 active FDE postings with disclosed compensation is $135,000 as of April 30, 2026. The average is $139,470. The full range is $49,920 to $365,000. Equity, sign-on bonuses, and OTE are not included in these figures.

Which companies are hiring the most FDEs right now?

Google leads with 34 active FDE postings as of April 30, 2026. Deloitte is second with 19. Amazon Web Services, Accenture, and Logic Inc. each have 3 active postings. Together the top 5 companies account for roughly 48% of all active FDE roles.

What tools do Forward Deployed Engineers use?

Based on tool mentions in 134 active FDE job descriptions, Python and GCP each appear in 53% of postings. AWS appears in 31%, Azure in 28%, and RAG (retrieval-augmented generation) in 22%. TypeScript, JavaScript, and prompt engineering round out the top tools. See the skills article for the full breakdown.

How does FDE Pulse collect market data?

FDE Pulse tracks job postings with 'Forward Deployed Engineer' in the title across major job boards. Salary data is extracted from postings that disclose compensation. Market intelligence signals (tools, team structure, geo focus) are derived from structured parsing of job description text. Data is updated weekly.
